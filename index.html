Our Story

Forged from
Coal Country.

Southeast Kansas didn't just mine coal — it built communities around it. The bakers who fed the miners. The cooks who kept the crews going. Iron & Embers carries that tradition forward.

Before the restaurant opens its doors, the truck rolls first. Homemade breads baked fresh every morning, breakfast made to order, and lunch worth driving across Crawford County for.

Every dish name comes from the mine. Every loaf is made from scratch. Nothing here comes from a bag or a box.
